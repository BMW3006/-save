## 🚀 **Features**

### 🎵 Media Downloader
- Download YouTube videos (MP4) and audio (MP3)
- Save TikTok videos without watermark
- Download Instagram Reels, Posts, and Stories
- Facebook video downloader
- Spotify to MP3 converter
- GitHub repository downloader

### 🤖 AI Studio
- GPT-4o Chat - Advanced AI conversations
- Gemini 3.5 Flash - Google's AI assistant
- AI Image Generator - Create images from text
- AI Song Generator - Generate music from prompts

### ⚽ Football Hub
- Live scores from leagues worldwide
- Premier League, La Liga, Bundesliga, Serie A
- League standings and tables
- UEFA Champions League standings
- Football news and updates
- Team and player search

### 🛠️ Tools Suite
- Stylish Text Generator (35+ text styles)
- QR Code Generator
- URL Shortener
- Instagram video downloader

### 📰 News Hub
- BBC News - World headlines
- Football news and transfers
- Technology news updates
